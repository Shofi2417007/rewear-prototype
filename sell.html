Tips for a great listing

Well-lit photos and honest descriptions help your item find the right new owner faster.

  • Take photos in natural light from different angles.
  • Mention any small marks, flaws or fit notes.
  • Choose “Gently used” or “Well loved” accurately.
  • Set a fair price or credit value based on condition.
