About Rewear

Rewear is a circular fashion platform designed to keep clothes in use for longer — helping people save money, express their style, and reduce environmental impact.

Our Story

Rewear was created with a simple goal: to make sustainable fashion accessible, affordable, and enjoyable for everyone. Every year, millions of clothes end up in landfills despite being in perfectly wearable condition. We believe that fashion shouldn’t cost the earth — literally.

Our platform allows people to give their clothes a second life by selling or swapping them through a community-driven, eco-friendly system. Whether you're looking to refresh your wardrobe or find unique pieces, Rewear makes it easy to shop consciously.

Our mission
To build a community where fashion is circular, waste is minimised, and every item is valued for as long as possible.

Why it matters

The fashion industry is one of the world’s biggest polluters. By swapping and reselling clothes, individuals can reduce carbon emissions, lower textile waste and choose mindful consumption over fast fashion.

With Rewear, sustainability meets style — in a way that’s simple, empowering, and good for our planet.
